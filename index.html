Warm-Up Drills are drills that get players moving around, getting their muscles warm and joints loose. Note that dynamic stretching is not inluded, these drills incorporate ball touches. Dynamic stretches should be the first thing players do when they walk into the gym.

Skill drills are specific to volleyball skills relating to defensive and offensive skills. Add a focus for the players to make these drills more realistic. However, start off slowly, getting these techniques right is better than focusing on the outcome.

Game drills are scrimmage drills that have some focus. Whether its siding out with the first ball or making consecutive points, the point is for players to play under some pressure.
